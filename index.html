About the Event

Previous Next

The wedding will take place at the Zeleny Les Residence, which is located at 2449 Scotch Hill Road in Bloomville, New York. Both the ceremony and the reception will be held here. Zeleny Les (Czech for "Green Woods") residence is located on 100 acres of rural conservation land in the Catskill Mountains. The wedding ceremony, which starts at 4 o'clock, will be officiated by Pastor Johnathon Gray of Albany, and will be held in the meadow below the Zeleny Les homesite. The ceremony site is about a 5-10 minute walk from the parking area but there will be a shuttle running back and forth for those who would prefer to ride down. The reception will be held in the terrace behind the residence. The events will be held primarily outdoors, but there will be patio warmers and indoor space available for those who would prefer it.
