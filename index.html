Justin & Amelia's Story

Amelia and Justin met on Labor Day Weekend of 2016 at Shenandoah National Park where they were attending a group camping trip with mutual friends. Their first adventure together was a 10 mile hike down a section of the Appalachian trail where Justin was amazed by Amelia’s golden retriever, Opie’s ability to dive into pools and retrieve rocks.

Soon after, Amelia and Justin developed a friendship and did a lot more camping and hiking trips together with their group of friends. Finally, in May of 2017 they went on a camping trip by themselves when their friends cancelled. They both had a lot of fun and they thought it might be nice to make a habit of spending time together away from the group.




Justin and Amelia started dating in June of 2017. Their relationship blossomed over their love of travel, hiking, camping, and of course, their dogs. Justin proposed on Labor Day weekend of 2018 beside the Michigan City lighthouse and pier… and Amelia, with Opie right beside her giving his blessing, said yes!

Since then, a lot has happened. Justin got a new job and they both moved from the Lexington, Kentucky area, to a small town right outside of Louisville, Kentucky, called Corydon. For the last year they have built a new home. Justin works as the Sterile Processing Manager at University of Louisville Hospital. Amelia works remotely as a Web Developer and also teaches English as a Second Language part-time. They have three dogs together- Opie the golden retriever, Barrett the German Shepherd, and the newest addition, Opie’s beautiful daughter, Scout. Amelia would like to have more golden retrievers, but Justin is not so sure.

Getting to Zeleny Les

Zelený Les is located in Bloomville, New York. It is roughly an hour and a half from Albany, 2 hours and 45 minutes from South Hadley, and a half hour from Oneonta. GPS will get you very close, if not directly in front of the venue. The address is:

2449 Scotch Hill Road
Bloomville, NY 13739

Travel & Lodging

There are many lodging options around the venue in Bloomville- including cabins, inns, and bed & breakfasts. If you are looking to stay in a hotel, the closest ones are about a half hour away in Oneonta.

The closest major airport to fly into is Albany International Airport.
