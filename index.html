About the Event

Previous Next
The wedding will take place at the Zelený Les Residence, which is located at 2449 Scotch Hill Road in Bloomville, New York. Both the ceremony and the reception will be held here. Zelený Les (Czech for "Green Woods") residence is located on 100 acres of rural conservation land in the Catskill Mountains. The wedding ceremony, which starts at 4 o'clock, will be officiated by Pastor Johnathon Gray of Albany, and will be held in the meadow below the Zelený Les homesite. The ceremony site is about a 5-10 minute walk from the parking area but there will be a shuttle running back and forth for those who would prefer to ride down. The reception will be held in the terrace behind the residence. The events will be held primarily outdoors, but there will be patio warmers and indoor space available for those who would prefer it.

Directions to the Venue

Zelený Les is located in Bloomville, New York, a tiny hamlet in Delaware County (pop. 926) and a 10-minute drive to the town of Delhi, the county seat, whose Main Street features a handful of eateries and long-established family businesses. By car, Zelený Les is roughly 1.5 hours from Albany, 2 hours and 45 minutes from South Hadley, and 1 hour from Cooperstown. GPS will get you very close, if not directly in front of the venue.
